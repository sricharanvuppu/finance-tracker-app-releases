# Finance Tracker — Releases

Android APK releases for **Finance Tracker**, a private, offline-first personal finance app
(expenses, budgets, lending, holdings, insights) with optional Google Drive backup.

Your data stays on your device. No ads, no mandatory accounts, nothing sent to any server.
Optional backup goes only to *your own* Google Drive's private app folder.

## 📥 Download & install

1. Open the [**Releases**](https://github.com/sricharanvuppu/finance-tracker-app-releases/releases) page and download the latest **`.apk`**.
2. Open it on your Android phone (**Android 7.0+**).
3. If prompted, allow **“Install unknown apps”** for your browser/Files app.
4. Tap **Install**. Installing over an older version **keeps all your existing data**.

## ✨ Features

- **Home dashboard** — Overview / Insights toggle: net worth, income, expense, savings rate, run-rate, and charts (trend, savings, category breakdown, needs vs wants, year-over-year).
- **Transactions** — quick add, **split** one payment across categories, date & time pickers, powerful filters (type, category, sub-category, event, date range), and tap-to-drill-down from any figure. Undo on delete.
- **Accounts, Lending, Holdings** — bank/cash/credit accounts; loans grouped by person with repayment timelines; investment holdings with average cost & gain/loss.
- **Budgets, recurring bills, life events & goals** — with optional budget alerts and bill reminders.
- **Light / Dark / System** themes.
- **Home-screen widgets** — Overview + one-tap Quick Add.
- **Security** — optional biometric / device-PIN app lock.
- **Backup** — one-tap Google Drive backup & restore, plus CSV/JSON import-export.

## 🔒 Privacy

All data is stored locally on your device. Google Drive backup (optional) uploads only to your
Google account’s private app-data folder. Nothing is shared with the developer or any third party.

## 📋 Requirements

- Android 7.0 (Nougat) or newer.
