# Finance Tracker — Releases

Android APK releases for **Finance Tracker**, a private, offline-first personal finance app
(expenses, budgets, loans, investments, insights) with optional Google Drive backup.

Your data stays on your device. No ads, no mandatory accounts, nothing sent to any server.
Optional backup goes only to *your own* Google Drive's private app folder.

## 📥 Download & install

1. Open the [**Releases**](https://github.com/sricharanvuppu/finance-tracker-app-releases/releases) page and download the latest **`.apk`**.
2. Open it on your Android phone (**Android 7.0+**).
3. If prompted, allow **"Install unknown apps"** for your browser/Files app.
4. Tap **Install**. Installing over an older version **keeps all your existing data**.

_The app also updates itself over-the-air on reopen, so you get fixes between APK releases._

## ✨ Features

- **Home dashboard** — Overview / Insights toggle.
  - *Overview*: net worth, **Cash & Bank**, and **Investments** (with **daily change**), each with a colour-bar breakdown.
  - *Insights*: plain-language **highlights**, a **savings-rate** KPI card, **spending vs the previous period** with **⚠️ >20% jump** alerts (works for any date range), recurring commitments, a savings-goal bar, expenses-by-category, and more charts on demand.
- **Transactions** — quick add, **split** one payment across categories, date & time pickers, powerful filters (type, category, event, date range), tap-to-drill-down, undo on delete.
- **Investments** — one unified list of **investment types** (Stock, Mutual Fund, Gold, FD, Chit…, each with its own icon); holdings with **live prices**, **day change**, average cost, **XIRR**, dividends, and sell/redeem.
- **Accounts & Loans** — bank/cash/credit accounts; personal loans (🟢 Lent / 🔴 Borrowed) and bank loans with EMIs; repayments entered by amount with account selection and an editable history.
- **Budgets, recurring bills, life events & goals** — with optional budget alerts and bill reminders.
- **Light / Dark / System** themes · **hide-amounts** privacy toggle · home-screen widgets (Overview + Quick Add).
- **Security** — optional biometric / device-PIN app lock.
- **Backup** — one-tap Google Drive backup & restore, plus CSV/JSON import-export.

## 🔒 Privacy

All data is stored locally on your device. Google Drive backup (optional) uploads only to your
Google account's private app-data folder. Nothing is shared with the developer or any third party.

## 📋 Requirements

- Android 7.0 (Nougat) or newer.
